📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a real-world hotel booking dataset containing nearly 1,19,390 bookings across two hotel types — City Hotel and Resort Hotel. The goal is to identify key patterns in bookings, cancellations, pricing, and customer behavior to help hotel management make data-driven decisions.

🔍 Key Findings
🏙️ City Hotel receives significantly more bookings than Resort Hotel
📅 August is the peak arrival month — summer is the busiest season
🍳 Bed & Breakfast (BB) is the most preferred meal plan
🌍 Portugal (PRT) is the top country of origin for guests
💻 Online Travel Agencies dominate the booking channel
❌ 37% of bookings are cancelled — a major revenue concern
📈 Higher prices lead to more cancellations — pricing is a key factor
🇵🇹 Portugal accounts for 70% of all reservation cancellations
📆 January has the highest number of cancelled reservations
